Welcome to MarketPlace: Your Solution to reducing Food Waste and Unlocking the Value of Unwanted Food.

Our Goal

The primary goal of our project is to create a web application that serves as a platform for connecting food producers (such as farm owners), individuals, restaurants, and other relevant institutions. The overarching aim is to reduce food wastage and promote the efficient utilization of surplus or unwanted food resources.

Objective

A user-friendly web application with robust functionality enables food producers, individuals, restaurants, and institutions to easily connect and collaborate.

Green Market Place streamlines the redistribution of surplus, expired, or spoiled food from restaurants and cooperatives. Furthermore, it benefits farmers by providing valuable resources for livestock food and fertilizer production. Join us in reducing food waste and supporting sustainability.
